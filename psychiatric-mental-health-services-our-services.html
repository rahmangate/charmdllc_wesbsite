Who We Serve

We provide services for children, adolescents, and older adults. With our support, they will be provided with a safe and welcoming environment where they will be guided in addressing their substance abuse and mental health issues and improve the quality of their lives. The individuals we support may be experiencing relationship issues, life challenges, behavioral issues with a child or teen, mental illness, and/or a substance use problem.

We provide mental and behavioral health services for the following:

  • Anxiety Disorder
  • Depressive Disorders
  • Mood Disorders
  • Trauma and Stressor Related Disorders
  • Psychotic Disorders

Charmd Behavioral Healthcare also provides the following:

  • Psychiatric assessment and diagnosis
  • Individual, group, and family therapy
  • Medication management
  • Couples and marital counseling
  • Family psychoeducation
  • Play therapy
  • Safe outpatient detoxification
  • Drug and alcohol use support
  • Drug and alcohol education
